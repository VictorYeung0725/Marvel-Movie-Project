Hawkeye's Bow and Quiver are a pair of specialized tools used by former S.H.I.E.L.D. agent and Avenger, Hawkeye. Serving as his primary weapons, Hawkeye carries a unique, collapsible recurve bow along with a mechanized quiver that stores and deploys his trick arrows.

Weapon

Ronin's Retractable Sword is a sword used by Clint Barton during his time as Ronin to assassinate criminals who had survived the Snap. After being used to fight the Outriders during the Battle of Earth, the sword was left in the ruins of the Avengers Compound. It was later recovered and intended for sale on the black market, although it was stolen by Jack Duquesne. Barton encountered Duquesne shortly after, and regaining possession over the sword, used it during his fight with Echo.
